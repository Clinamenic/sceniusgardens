The Sensemaking Scenius

The internet gods

blessed us

with an abundance

of information &

connectivity

Something about yourself

and in the process,

boiled our brains

we’re lost,

in a swirl of irrelevancy

trading our attention,

at too low a price

Technology has

destroyed

our collective

sensemaking

It’s time to

rebuild our sanity.

but

how?

introducing

The

Sensemaking

Scenius

a community of practice

for digital builders, researchers, artists & activists

who share a vision of a

regenerative

intentional

&

meaningful

internet

The Scenius is our online abode

where we cultivate

collective intelligence & wisdom

by tending to a permaculture of

relational technologies
